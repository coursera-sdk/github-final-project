1. All contributors are expected to treat others with respect and professionalism at all times.
2. Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior of any kind will not be tolerated.
3. Be constructive when providing feedback, and focus on improving the project collaboratively.
4. Respect differing opinions, experiences, and perspectives within the community.
5. Use welcoming and inclusive language in all communications.
6. Report any unacceptable behavior to the project maintainers promptly.
7. Maintain a positive environment that encourages learning and growth.
8. Avoid personal attacks and instead address issues with empathy and clarity.
9. Follow all applicable laws and ethical guidelines while contributing.
10. Violations of this code may result in warnings, removal, or banning from the project.
